POWERSCAPE ACE TECH PRIVATE LIMITED Company Profile Powerscape Ace Tech Private Limited is a technology-driven infrastructure and specialized construction company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with its registered office in New Delhi, India. The company is established with the vision of delivering reliable, sustainable, and future-ready solutions in the fields of energy, infrastructure development, and technical services. The organization focuses on integrating engineering expertise, modern project management practices, and innovative technologies to support the growing demand for renewable energy and infrastructure projects across India. With a strong emphasis on quality, compliance, and long-term partnerships, Powerscape Ace Tech Private Limited aims to become a trusted execution and facilitation partner for developers, investors, and government agencies. Core Business Areas The company operates in specialized construction and technical services, with a growing focus on renewable and green energy infrastructure. Its primary business verticals include: • Land aggregation and site facilitation for solar and wind power projects • Technical feasibility studies and site suitability analysis • Infrastructure development and project support services • Coordination with local authorities, landowners, and regulatory bodies • EPC support services and vendor coordination • Renewable energy project planning and documentation assistance Vision To become a leading facilitator and solution provider in India’s renewable energy and infrastructure ecosystem by enabling sustainable development through innovation, transparency, and reliable project execution. Mission To deliver end-to-end technical and operational support for clean energy and infrastructure projects by combining strong local networks, professional governance, and advanced technical capabilities, ensuring value creation for all stakeholders. Strengths and Value Proposition Powerscape Ace Tech Private Limited differentiates itself through a practical, on-ground approach supported by structured corporate governance. The company’s key strengths include: • Strong understanding of land acquisition and stakeholder management in rural and semi-urban regions • Focus on regulatory compliance and documentation accuracy • Agile project support model tailored to developers and investors • Emphasis on sustainable development and environmental responsibility • Transparent communication with landowners, partners, and clients Corporate Governance The company is managed by experienced directors and follows statutory compliance requirements as prescribed by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. It maintains a professional framework for financial reporting, regulatory filings, and operational governance to ensure accountability and trust with its partners and clients. Growth Strategy Powerscape Ace Tech Private Limited is positioned to scale its operations in the renewable energy and infrastructure sector by expanding its network of landowners, developers, and technical partners. The company aims to strengthen its presence in high-potential solar and wind corridors across multiple Indian states while continuously upgrading its technical and project management capabilities. Commitment to Sustainability The company aligns its operations with national and global sustainability goals by actively supporting clean energy projects that contribute to carbon reduction, rural development, and long-term environmental conservation. Summary Powerscape Ace Tech Private Limited is an emerging professional enterprise focused on supporting India’s renewable energy and infrastructure growth through specialized construction services, land facilitation, and technical project support. With a forward-looking approach, strong governance framework, and commitment to sustainable development, the company strives to build long-term value for developers, investors, communities, and strategic partners.
